History of Dobermans

Dobermans originated in Germany primarily used as guard dogs. Their exact ancestry is unknown but are believed to be a mixture of a few different dog breeds. These breeds include Rottweiler, Black and Tan Terrier, and German Pincsher. Dobermans have a sleek coat, cropped ears, docked tail, and an athletic build. Dobermans are highly energetic and very intelligent, perfect for military and police work as well as a family guard dog and companion.

Dobermans have come a long way from the fierce dog created by Louis Dobermann. Today’s Doberman is an affectionate, loving family dog. They are not for people who want a dog to look at from a distance. Dobie’s are intensely loyal to their families and demand attention. A Dobie’s priority in life is to be with you, love you and guard you 24 hours a day.
